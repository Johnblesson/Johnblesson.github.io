Welcome to Alx Portfolio Project.

This project contain a simple Currency Converter and a Stopwatch.

The Currency Converter and the Stopwatch project is designed to
provide you with a versatile toolset for both currency conversion and time tracking.
Whether you're a traveler, business professional, or simply need to keep track of time,
the project offers a seamless and efficient solution.

CURRENCY CONVERTER

The Currency Converter allows you to effortlessly convert
between different currencies. With support for a wide range
of global currencies, you can quickly obtain accurate exchange
rates and perform conversions in real-time. Stay informed
about currency fluctuations and make informed decisions for
your international transactions.

STOPWATCH

The Stopwatch feature offers a reliable and precise
time tracking solution. You need to measure elapsed
time for workouts, events, or any other activity,
our Stopwatch provides an intuitive interface for
starting, pausing, and resetting the timer.
Keep track of time with ease and accuracy.
